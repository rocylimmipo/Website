FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I look for an item on AAMARI website?

There are several ways to browse AAMARI. You may explore our weekly homepage features or visit the navigation bar found at the top of every page. If you are looking for a specific item, you may use the search box in order to find the products by name, color, category or style. Visit us to explore the details and inspirations behind the success of AAMARI collections as well as an exclusive insight into the fashion world.


Are the products sold online new?

Our company guarantees that all products featured and sold in the market are always new. For an addded information, our webiste only offers a preview on the items that we sell in our official stores worldwide. You may only view the product itself with the help of our website.


Why the price and sizes are not displayed on the site?

Considering our price and sizes being strictly displayed on our website; in order to buy and order the product within the reach store of our willig buyers;and having a connection with the main website of AAMARI will help as well.


How can I identify a fake AAMARI website?

AAMARI products are frequently offered for sale on websites published at domain names containing the AAMARI trademark. Having the authenticity and details of a real website can tell the difference between the official and fake website.


How do I repair an item coming from the purchased product of AAMARI?

For AAMARI products being purchased in any legit and AAMARI-authorized retailer, refer directly to the official customer service team or contact us immediately.


How do I exchange my order?

We do not offer direct exchanges with the item. If you would like to exchange goods, we ask you to simply place a new order and present the official receipt on the day you bought the same product.
