ABOUT THE SITE

Fashion serves as a platform to express someone’s identity. Welcome to AAMARI, your number one source for all things. Launched in 1978,AAMARI offers a wide range of apparel to fit everyone’s unique sense of fashion style.

Our clothing and accessories strives to provide you the latest fashions. To help you in style, we post new arrivals daily, as well as offer stylist picks to help any indecisive shoppers.

AAMARI is every fashionista’s best place to create their perfect wardrobe. Beyond helping you look your best and providing unique kind of fashion styles, we endeavour to make every purchase a positive and fun experience.

AAMARI aims not just to help you find your own style. We are more than a brand for we have a purpose. In every purchase of any item, you are giving a contribution in our foundation which is the AAMARI Child Foundation. A foundation that helps the abandoned children.

If you are a first-time visitor or long-standing customer, we hope you enjoy our products as we enjoy offering them to you.

THE FACE BEHIND AAMARI

Giovanni Aamari is an Italian fashion designer. He is known for his excellent and timeless designs. Aamari was born on July 7, 1937 in Verona, Italy. Aamari grew up in a wealthy family. While growing up, Aamari is already passionate in drawing and designing clothes, however it only remain as a hobby, for he always wanted to be a doctor. He enrolled in the Department of Medicine at the Biological Institutes of the Medical School, University of Verona. After 3 years he decided to drop out from the medical school and shifted to another courses but he always end up failing.

One day he dreamt about winning an award as a fashion designer. From that day on, he get back to his passion which is drawing and designing clothes. In mid-1960s he started designing for Era’s apparel. With such talent he also free-lanced to other companies and manufacturers. In the late 1960s he was invited to different to fashion shows in different countries such as France, Beijing, New York and London. This led him to gain praises and acknowledgement for his works. The experiences provided Aamari to create his own styles. He was now ready to devote his efforts to his own label, and on his 44th birthday on July 7, 1978, he founded his 1st branch of AAMARI at his home town in Verona, Italy. In December of that same year, he released his first collection of winter outfits for both men and women. From then on, his brand paved its way across the country and the world and is continuously producing unique and high end sense of fashion. He is known today as one of the most successful Italian designers with 19 branches across the world and an annual turnover of $2 billion and a personal fortune of $9 billion as of 2017. At the age of 81 Giovanni Aamari has already and continuing making history in the fashion industry.
